Artist

Yumok流木 Lee Dong-joo(李東周, 1952-2024)

He served for 46 years in the Department of Civil Engineering at Kunsan National University, including as professor, dedicating himself to nurturing the next generation of scholars, and was awarded the Order of Civil Merit with Yellow Stripes. After retirement, he pursued photography, creating works of profound philosophy and artistry centered on water and light.

His photographs capture the fleeting landscapes created by driftwood dancing in the harmony of light and water. We invite you to appreciate the works of the late Lee Dong-joo, who expressed the pure and transient beauty of nature through poetic and artistic photography, and hope they inspire you deeply.
